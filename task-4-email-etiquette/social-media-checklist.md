Social Media Do’s and Dont's

Do’s:
1. Use strong and unique passwords
2. Keep your profiles professional
3. Verify information before sharing
4. Respect others online
5. Adjust privacy settings properly

Dont's:
1. Do not share personal information publicly
2. Do not click on unknown links
3. Do not post inappropriate content
4. Do not share OTP or passwords
5. Do not engage in online harassment
