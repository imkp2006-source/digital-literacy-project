Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
2. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
3. Always verify payment requests before approving
4. Use trusted and official apps for transactions
5. Enable two-factor authentication where possible
6. Regularly check your bank account statements
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
8. Immediately report fraud on cybercrime.gov.in
9. Call helpline number 1930 in case of any fraud
